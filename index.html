Remote Tutoring Services

Our mission is to help students succeed by building on what they already know. Students are encouraged to communicate their knowledge. This supports learning by allowing them to try to solve problems and questions first, instead of relying on outside help. Their learning is then supported by teaching and instruction to help them build accurate and effective conceptual models for their topics.

Stéphane [stɛ fan] lives in Cochrane, AB in the foothills of the Rockies. He studies Computer Science at the University of Calgary and specializes in Software Engineering.

He graduated from Cochrane High School in 2012 and began tutoring in 2013. Over the years, he has developed his ability to simplify concepts, identify student needs, and teach problem solving skills. He is passionate about fostering understanding, critical thinking, and empathy in himself and in others.

Stéphane is an avid rock climber, guitarist, and cook. He enjoys spending time in the mountains, gaming with friends, and working on personal projects.
